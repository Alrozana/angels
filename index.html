From Local Vision to Global Impact

DataQueue is an Al startup transforming how businesses leverage voice and data intelligence, with advanced platforms that automate communication and deliver actionable insights. Founded by Bashir Alsaifi, the company has expanded from Palestine into international markets, addressing Arabic and multilingual Al needs. Its combination of visionary leadership, innovative technology, and scalable solutions positions it as a compelling opportunity for strategic partners and investors.

Starting a startup in Palestine is challenging due to limited resources, especially early-stage funding. Access to finance is crucial for founders to focus on solutions and customer needs. Venture capital demands traction and validation, which require initial funding typically from friends, family, and angel investors. I believe that having an angel network like Flow Angels can significantly support entrepreneurs and boost venture capital deals.
